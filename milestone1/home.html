Website Summary


What this website will contain?
This website will go into the details about how foods
can affect people, as well as how they can use food for energy and exercise.

Where will the content come from?

The content will come from journal sources and factual media on the web.

Why should users care about the site?

Users should care about the site because it will offer necessary information
for people to use in their daily lives to stay healthy.

Why should a site be built for this?

A site should be built for this to allow people to read important information about how food can affect everyone.

What would you like users to do at your site?

Users should be able to read articles and information about food and nutrition. This can also be used for businesses to use this information to sell and market specific products food related.

What is the goal of this site?

The goal for this site is to allow people to understand how food works in the body, and to allow businesses
to showcase some products and market them (food related).
